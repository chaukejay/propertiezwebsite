Cookie Policy for Propertiez.co.za

Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to provide comprehensive information about the use of cookies on Propertiez.co.za.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owners.

What cookies are used for?

There are several types of cookies used on Propertiez.co.za:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the operation of the website.
  • Functional cookies: Remember choices you make and provide enhanced features.
  • Analytical cookies: Help us analyze how visitors use our website.
  • Advertising cookies: Used to deliver advertisements relevant to your interests.

Types of cookies

There are two main types of cookies:

  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a specified period.
  • Session cookies: Temporary and deleted when you close your browser.

Managing cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings or using cookie management tools. Learn more about managing cookies.

Links to third-party websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their cookie policies, so please review them before providing any personal information.

Updates to this policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice on our website.
